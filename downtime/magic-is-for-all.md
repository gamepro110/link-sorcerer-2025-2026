# Magic is for All

Prerequisite: 3 attuned items of different rarity

It is the dream of many an artificer that magic is not something for the few who study or who are born with innate magical ability or the favour of a god or something else.
It is thus that methods to attune to more magic items are needed.
For now this requires training and meditation but soon attunement might be a thing of the past.
You gain 1 additional attunement slot.

Once you stop being attuned to 3 magic items of different rarities, this feature stops working until you meet the prerequisites again.
