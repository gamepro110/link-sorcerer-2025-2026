# Chronology Watch
Rare
requires attunement

This small golden watch is silent, seemingly broken.
Yet when a spell is cast by the attuned creature it can be stored inside instead.
While it contains the spell the watches some alive ticking violently and its arms spin in opposite directions.

Whomever holds the watch can use an action to cast the spell stored inside as though they are the caster.
This spell is cast using the spell save DC and spellcasting bonus of the creature attuned to this item.
