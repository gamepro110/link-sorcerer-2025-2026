# Wrath-Fused Amulet of Hecate

Wondrous item, requires attunement by spellcaster

A bronze amulet adorned with the icons of two crossed torches, that of Hecate.
The magic, spite, and wrath oozing from it fill you with magical power, and the goddess's magical nature starts its work anew.
Distant whispers of a slain dragon fill the wearer's head, may their company be curse or blessing. 
You can use the amulet as a spellcasting focus for your spells while wearing or holding it, and you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your sorcerer spells of +2.
When you use a Metamagic option on a spell while you are holding or wearing the amulet, you can momentarily curse one creature targeted by the spell; choose one ability score, and until the end of your next turn, the creature has disadvantage on ability checks and saving throws that use that ability.
Once per long rest, at the end of your turn as a free action, you can end all Conditions affecting you.
When you do so, you take 1d6 psychic damage for each Condition affecting you, as you resolve your inner conflicts. 

As an action, you can expend a spell slot of 3th level or higher to touch a willing creature.
For 8 hours, the target's skin has a rough, scale-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 17 + the target's dex, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.
